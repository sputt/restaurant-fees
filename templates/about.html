I Dislike Fees

My name is Spencer Putt. I built this page to try to help put an end to this ridiculous fee practice in some small way. The data comes from Reddit, visits to restaurants, and other personal accounts.

Contributions

Please open a pull request on Github or email me to add (or God willing, remove) a restaurant.

Contact

Send inquiries to fees.restaurant@gmail.com

To restaurants - please send corrections. I will happily include your point of view in your restaurant's fee page. If you found any of my coverage in the FAQ unfair, please email me to discuss.
